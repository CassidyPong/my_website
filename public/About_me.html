Artist Bio

Born and raised in the bay area, Cassidy Pong is an interdisciplinary digital media artist recognized for her complex and ambitious works. Currently, she is a undergraduate student at San Jose State University expecting to receive her BFA in Digital Media Art in the Fall of 2019. From a young age, she has always been captivated by the art world and strives to become a digital media artist in the future. Growing up, she enjoyed doing gymnastics, dancing, and playing video games, ultimately inspiring her to one day work in the gaming industry or starting her own company in the digital field.

Artist Statement

I often think of my artwork as an extension of myself. Throughout my life, it has always been a medium in which I convey my personal thoughts and emotions that I have felt and experienced. By placing myself into my work, I am able to make a better connection to myself, my practice, and to my audience. My work mainly focuses on uncomfortable subjects including topics such as depression, the loss of a loved one, and suicide, however, I also like to explore anything that peaks my interest or experiment with different mediums and ideas. The subject matter of each body of work determines what materials I use or what the focus of the work will be. I work in a variety of different mediums but mainly focus on creating digital artwork including digital imagery, video, web development, projection mapping, and game design. However, I also create physical artworks, such as 3D printing, traditional drawing and painting, and 3D sculpture.
